    DIVINITE

    Our project named "Beauty_Spa" consists of the development of a responsive e-commerce portal for the company DIVINITE which sells spa services and feminine beauty such as nails, eyelashes, hairstyles, skin treatments, among others.

    The project began with a design and prototyping proposal where the colors to be used are exposed, as well as the sections of home, about us, services (catalog), log in, profile, suggestion box, payment, and reservations.

    We worked using the Agile methodology for 6 weeks, and collaborating on GitHub.
